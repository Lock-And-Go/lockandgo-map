What is LockAndGo?
A discovery platform for luggage storage in Bratislava, Slovakia. We aggregate every viable storage spot in the city onto one map with prices, ratings and opening hours. Tap a spot → open its details → book directly with the operator.
Where can I store my luggage in Bratislava?
16 storage spots across Bratislava — most are in the Old Town, near the Main Train Station (Hlavná stanica), at OC Nivy, on Štúrova street and in Petržalka. The mix includes staffed shops, hotels and cafés plus self-service 24/7 lockers. Each spot has its own booking — some via global networks, others directly with the operator.
How much does luggage storage cost in Bratislava?
Prices start from €5.90 per bag per day. Self-service lockers at OC Nivy start from €4 for 3 hours; the staffed left-luggage office at the Main Train Station starts from €2 per day. The final price is shown by the operator before payment. LockAndGo charges no markup.
Is there luggage storage at Bratislava Main Train Station?
Yes. The Main Train Station (Hlavná stanica) has both a staffed left-luggage office (around 06:15 – 20:00, from €2/day) and 24/7 self-service lockers on platform 1 (from €2 per locker). Several Bounce-partner shops and hotels around the station also offer storage with staff (typically 07:00 – 22:00).
Are there 24/7 luggage lockers in Bratislava?
Yes. 24/7 self-service lockers are available at OC Nivy shopping centre, at Bratislava Main Train Station (platform 1) and at Obchodná 9 in the Old Town. Most other storage spots are staffed shops and cafés with limited opening hours — the map shows live status ("Open now" / "Opens at…") for each.
Is luggage storage in Bratislava safe?
Yes. All listed spots are real businesses — shops, hotels and cafés with staff, or secured self-service lockers with CCTV. Some Bounce-partner operators include luggage insurance up to €10,000 per bag.
How do I book a luggage storage spot?
Tap a spot on the map, open its details and click "Book" — this takes you to the operator's booking page where you complete payment and receive a confirmation or PIN code. Self-service lockers are unlocked via QR code or PIN.
