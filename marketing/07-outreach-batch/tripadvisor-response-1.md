## Tripadvisor Bratislava Forum — Response Template 1

**Scenario:** Traveller is asking about luggage storage during a day trip from Vienna or Budapest. Standard thread shape: "We arrive at 10am, train out at 6pm, where can we leave 2 large suitcases."

**Etiquette notes:**
- Tripadvisor mods delete posts that read like ads. Lead with the answer, mention the site once near the end, and never paste the URL in the first sentence.
- Sign off as a name, not a brand. Use "Šimon" or "S. Kališ", not "LockAndGo Team".
- Reply to a real recent thread. If the OP has already booked something, congratulate them and skip the pitch.

---

**Response (150–220 words):**

```
For a Vienna day-trip with two large suitcases, you have three sensible options at the moment, in roughly the order I'd consider them.

1. The ZSSK staffed left-luggage counter inside the main railway station (Hlavná stanica) — €2 per bag per day for anything up to 15kg, €2.50 above that. It's the cheapest by a wide margin. Open around 6:15 to 20:00 with short breaks, cash only. Hand the bags in, take the ticket, you're done in two minutes.

2. The self-service lockers on platform 1 at the same station, 24/7. From €2, but the coin slot only accepts €1 and €2 pieces — no notes, no card. Bring change before you arrive.

3. If you'd rather book in advance and want English signage plus insurance, the Bounce and Radical Storage partners in the Old Town run €5–€5.90 per day. The closest one to the station is the "Near Bratislava Station" shop with 24/7 availability.

I keep an up-to-date map of all 32 storage spots in the city at lockandgo.sk/en — it's a side project I started this year, no booking fees added. Hope it helps. Have a good day in Bratislava.

— Šimon
```
