**Response (160–230 words):**

```
Late-night arrivals at Nivy or the main station are easier than they look, but the pricing matrix is confusing if you haven't seen it.

For overnight storage:
- **Nivy bus station** has self-service boxes on level −1, opposite the Alza shop. Open 24/7, card payment. A medium box (50×53×70 cm) is €8 for 24 hours, a large one €9. One important detail nobody mentions: if you leave a bag past 72 hours, staff move it to a back-room storage and recovery costs €30 plus €10 per extra day. For a single overnight you're fine.
- **Hlavná stanica self-service lockers** on platform 1 are 24/7 from around €2 — but coins only (€1 and €2 pieces). If you're arriving with cash from an ATM, you'll have notes and the McDonald's in the station hall is your best chance to break them.
- **Most SNP** (under the bridge, Petržalka side) has a small 24/7 locker bank with CCTV. WhatsApp support runs on +421 905 599 053, useful if a unit jams at 1am.

If you'd rather hand the bag to a human, the **Safestay hostel on Leskova 9A** has 24h reception and takes non-guest luggage for a fee — worth a phone call to confirm before walking over.

I keep a map of all the city's storage options at lockandgo.sk/en, with hours and prices per spot. Side project, no booking fees on top. Safe travels.

— Šimon
```
